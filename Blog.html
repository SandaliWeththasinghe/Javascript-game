The Paper Tower and the Helium Stick 

 The Paper Tower

On the 13hof June for our GNCT course we made a paper tower. The activity was done by 3 groups. Our Madam gave us a six different colored A4 sheets and a sellotape roll and asked us to build a tower from that given limited materials in a given time period. 
To  win the tower should be stable and should be the highest. 



At the beginning the given task was hard for all three groups. But at the end all the three groups made fine paper towers.











                                                                                                                   





After making towers our Madam checked the stability of the towers.












There are some other photos of the activity.
 
 



















The Helium Stick

On the same day we had another activity called Helium Stick. Madam gave us a long stick. We had to rise that stick from the ground and hold it in the same height using a one finger by the all group members. 

Those two activities were very enjoyable and we had a great time on that day.
